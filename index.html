About Me

Northern born, Midwestern raised, southern-leaning, Nashville transplant. Like many, I migrated to music city to develop my skills as a musician- which culminated in my becoming a music professor and community director at Tennessee State University, a performer, and a composer of original music. While music is still a passion of mine, I found myself curious to create in a new medium after I saw a close friend make the career change to software development. She nudged me towards some introductory material and like a catchy tune. I was hooked. Coding unifies and satisfy my need for creativity, logic, and collaboration. What I love most is that like music, I have the opportunity to take the smallest of elements and build intricate works that can help influence so many others.

Projects

SongPlan

Individual

SongPlan is a single page app that allows music educators to create, edit, save, and share their various song plans. This app allow users to digitize and organize educational songs, enter them into a public database, that other users can consult freely and easily. Technologies used React, Javascript, BootStrap, ReactStrap, JSON, Postman, Firebase.

KewLyrics

Team of two

Can you sing a few lines of the melody but just cannot remember the song? We have an app for that. KewLyrics is a single page app that allows users to place in the words to a forgotten song and it will pull on songs that utilize those words. User can even print the lyrics to take it with them. Technologies used Javascript, BootStrap, JSON, Postman, MusixMatch.

Nutstalgia/Nutshell React

Team of four

Nutstalgia is an application that displays a user's friends, tasks, events, messages, and news all pertaining to the user's life. User can request, approve, and deny friends. Users are also able to create, edit, delete, save, and organize each aspect as needed. The target audience is senior citizens. Technologies used HTML, CSS, Javascript, BootStrap, JSON.
